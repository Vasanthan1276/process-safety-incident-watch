Under Review means the event appears relevant but the strongest source currently scores below the confirmed-source threshold. These items are retained instead of being discarded and can move to Confirmed when stronger corroboration appears.
Automated discovery is a screening tool and is not a substitute for formal incident investigation or source verification. Reliability and relevance scores should be reviewed before formal internal distribution.
